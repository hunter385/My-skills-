## The reframe

A team doesn't fail because it doesn't know what to do this year. It fails because it doesn't know what to do this week.

Annual plans drift. Quarterly priorities float. The level where work actually happens is the week and the day. Weekly 3 / Daily 3 closes that gap — it forces every person on the team to translate Cycle Rocks into "what I'm doing right now to move this forward."

## The framework

**Weekly 3:** Three things this week that move my Cycle Goal forward. Set during a Monday-morning Preview.

**Daily 3:** Three things today that move the Weekly 3 forward. Set during the morning's Preview.

Each evening: Review. What got done. What didn't. What carries.
Each Friday: Review. What moved. What's next week's Weekly 3.

## How to use it

**The Daily Preview + Review rhythm:**

- **Morning (5 minutes):** Look at the Weekly 3. Pick the Daily 3 that move it forward. Block calendar time for each.
- **Evening (5 minutes):** Did the Daily 3 get done? What carries to tomorrow? What needs to be re-prioritized?

**The Weekly Preview + Review rhythm:**

- **Friday or Monday (15 minutes):** Look at the Cycle Goals. Pick the Weekly 3 that move them forward. Schedule the week around them.
- **End of week (15 minutes):** Review what moved. Note what's blocked. Brief the team during the Cycle review.

**The two rules:**

1. **Three. Not five.** Five priorities means no priorities. Three is the limit because three is what a human actually executes.
2. **Tied to a Cycle Goal.** If a Weekly 3 item doesn't trace back to a 365 Cycle Goal, it's a distraction. Capture it for later or delete it.

## Why this is the load-bearing daily layer

Everything else in the RSG operating system is upstream:

- Growth Plan tells you the year.
- Cycle Rocks tell you the quarter.
- 365 tells you your role.
- **Weekly 3 / Daily 3 is where the work actually gets done.**

Skip this layer and the team has a plan but no traction.

## The Preview / Review pairing

The Weekly 3 / Daily 3 system isn't just a planning tool — it's a learning tool. The Review is where drift gets caught early.

- Daily Review caught daily: small drift, easy fix.
- Weekly Review caught weekly: medium drift, manageable fix.
- Cycle Review caught at Cooldown: large drift, structural fix.

The earlier you catch drift, the cheaper the fix. Preview + Review is the daily-cadence early-warning system.
