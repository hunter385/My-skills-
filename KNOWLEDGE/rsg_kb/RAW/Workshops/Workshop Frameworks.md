# Workshop Agenda 

1. **INTRO:** open the workshop with energy and excitement, setting the stage for what's to come and getting people genuinely enthusiastic about diving into the topic at hand
2. **INVITE:** I'm hosting this workshop because I love helping our clients able to [ ]. 
	This is just one of many things we explore inside of of ___ , I'd love to have you join us in Black Belt this month. Just drop a "BB" in the comments if you're curious, saying, this is great, but i’d like to go deeper… and I'll share what we're working on—no pressure at all.
3. **Lesson 1**
4. **Lesson 2**
5. **Lesson 3**
6. **INVITE:** extend another invitation to join you in continuing this work together, making it clear what the next step looks like for those who are ready
7. **WRAP UP:** bring everything together with a strong close that reinforces the key takeaways and leaves participants feeling equipped and motivated
8. **PARTY:** we host a live question and answer session where we celebrate wins, work through challenges together, and help people apply what they've learned directly to their real situations
