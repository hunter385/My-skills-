# The Framework
---
**6-WEEK CYCLE**
_CONTENT PLANNING_



## Core Idea
We don't plan content forever. We run **6-week experiments.**

Here's why this works: six weeks is long enough to see real patterns, and short enough that you're not locked into something that isn't working. It removes the pressure of a permanent content strategy and replaces it with a low-stakes test — **did this work or didn't it?**

This is how you build **[[Brandwidth]]** without burning out. You're not chasing the algorithm. You're testing your way to clarity — learning what actually connects with the people you're trying to reach.


## When To Use
- Cooldown planning the next cycle
