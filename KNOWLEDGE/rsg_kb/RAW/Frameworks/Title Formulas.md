# 🧲 Title & Hook Formulas

## 1. How-To (No Pain)
**Formula:**  
How to [Result] without [Pain]

**Example:**  
How to double your revenue without hiring a bigger team

**Why it works:**  
Clear outcome + removes fear

---

## 2. Time-Bound Transformation
**Formula:**  
How to [Result] in [Timeframe]

**Example:**  
How to build a profitable funnel in 30 days

**Why it works:**  
Speed + specificity

---

## 3. Mechanism Revelation
**Formula:**  
The [Mechanism] that lets you [Result]

**Example:**  
The 3-page funnel that lets you close high-ticket clients automatically

**Why it works:**  
Shifts focus to the vehicle

---

## 4. Big Domino
**Formula:**  
The one [Thing/System] that [Outcome]

**Example:**  
The one system that turns cold traffic into buyers

**Why it works:**  
Signals leverage + simplicity

---

## 5. Mistake Breaker
**Formula:**  
Why [Belief] is killing your [Result]

**Example:**  
Why more traffic is killing your funnel profits

**Why it works:**  
Pattern interrupt + belief shift

---

## 6. Identity Shift
**Formula:**  
From [Current State] to [Desired Identity]

**Example:**  
From overwhelmed coach to predictable revenue machine

**Why it works:**  
Identity transformation

---

## 7. Step Framework
**Formula:**  
The [X]-step blueprint to [Result]

**Example:**  
The 5-step blueprint to a scalable value ladder

**Why it works:**  
Structured + safe

---

## 8. If / Then
**Formula:**  
If you want [Result], you must [Insight]

**Example:**  
If you want high-ticket sales, you must stop selling

**Why it works:**  
Creates tension + curiosity

---

## 9. Hidden Truth
**Formula:**  
The hidden reason you’re not [Result] — and how to fix it

**Example:**  
The hidden reason you’re not converting — and how to fix it

**Why it works:**  
Diagnoses + resolves pain

---

## 10. Case Study
**Formula:**  
How we [Result] using [Mechanism]

**Example:**  
How we built a breakeven funnel in 7 days using hook, story, offer

**Why it works:**  
Proof + authority

---
