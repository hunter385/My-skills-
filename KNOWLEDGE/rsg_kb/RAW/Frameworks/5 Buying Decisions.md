# The Framework
---
![[3. Resources/Excalidraw/5 Buying Decisions|5 Buying Decisions]]




## Core Idea
Buyers have to navigate and walk through each of the 5 buying decisions before saying yes.




**YOU**: 
- do I like you
- trust you
- respect
- and believe you... the person in front of them?


**COMPANY**:
- do I like you
- trust you
- respect
- and believe the company you represent?


**PRODUCT**: 
- I get that your product works for others... but will it work for me?


**VALUE**: 
- is it in my budget? Can i even afford it?
- is there a better use case that I can invest the same amount of money into? (i could hire a staff member instead)

**TIMING**: 
- when should I do this?
- think parkensens law. if the deadline is far away, they'll take a long time to make that decision.
- if the deadline is at the end of the month or week, they'll shrink the decision time. 
- our job is to accelerate the deadlines and let them see the advantages to buying now.








# **3 TYPES OF OBJECTIONS**

1. **PRICE OBJECTION**: "i don't know if it's in my budget"
2. **PRODUCT OBJECTION**: "i'm not sure how that works, explain it to me again?"
3. **INDIFFERENCE**: "i think i need to go and speak to my partner..."
