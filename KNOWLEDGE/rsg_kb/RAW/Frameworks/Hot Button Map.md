## Core Idea
**HOT BUTTON MAP:** 
What are the motivators to get someone off their butt and take action?


<mark style="background: #FF5582A6;">Away from Pain</mark>

<mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">Towards Pleasure</mark>


**Bottom Left:** <mark style="background: #FF5582A6;">Frustrations:</mark> 
Things people want to **move away from**… **right now**
_if you don’t get this stuff sorted, these are the things you’re gonna be stuck frustrated with…._

**Bottom Right:** <mark style="background: #FF5582A6;">Fear:</mark> 
*If all the “frustrations” you mentioned are true, what's the long-term outcome they're scared will happen? (what happens in 12 months from now?)*

**Top Left:** <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">Wants:</mark> 
*Take your 3–5 bullet points from "frustrations" and flip them to the positive.*

**Top Right:** <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">Aspirations:</mark> 
*Take your 3–5 bullet points from "fear" and flip them to the positive. If they accomplish the stuff from the "wants," where will they end up in 12 months?*





## When To Use
- designing a workshop intro to hook the audience
- creating ads to promote the upcoming workshop
- needing written copy for a website
