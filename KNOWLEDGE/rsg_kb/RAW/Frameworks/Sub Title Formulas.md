# 🧩 Subtitle Formulas

## 1. Clarifier + Specific Outcome
**Formula:**  
Discover exactly how to [result] even if [obstacle]

**Example:**  
Discover exactly how to build a profitable funnel even if you don’t have an audience

**Why it works:**  
Clarity + removes resistance

---

## 2. Mechanism + Simplicity
**Formula:**  
Using a simple [system] that eliminates [pain]

**Example:**  
Using a simple 3-step value ladder system that eliminates guesswork

**Why it works:**  
Makes complexity feel easy

---

## 3. Speed + Efficiency
**Formula:**  
So you can [result] faster, easier, with less [pain]

**Example:**  
So you can close clients faster without chasing leads

**Why it works:**  
Emphasizes ease + speed

---

## 4. Identity Upgrade
**Formula:**  
Designed for [who] who want to become [identity]

**Example:**  
Designed for coaches who want to become category leaders

**Why it works:**  
Speaks to identity, not just outcome

---

## 5. Belief Breaker
**Formula:**  
Why what you’ve been told about [topic] is wrong — and what works

**Example:**  
Why more traffic isn’t the answer — and what actually scales revenue

**Why it works:**  
Breaks assumptions

---

## 6. Control + Predictability
**Formula:**  
Turn [chaos] into predictable [result]

**Example:**  
Turn inconsistent launches into predictable revenue cycles

**Why it works:**  
Promises stability

---

## 7. From → To + Mechanism
**Formula:**  
Move from [pain] to [transformation] with [mechanism]

**Example:**  
Move from scattered offers to a structured ascension system

**Why it works:**  
Clear transformation path

---

## 8. Quantified Outcome
**Formula:**  
Learn the exact steps to [measurable result]

**Example:**  
Learn the exact steps to increase average cart value by 30%

**Why it works:**  
Concrete + believable

---

## 9. Risk Reversal
**Formula:**  
No [pain], no [method], no [fear]

**Example:**  
No cold calling, no complicated tech, no massive ad budgets

**Why it works:**  
Removes objections

---

## 10. Future-Paced Benefit
**Formula:**  
So you can finally [aspirational outcome]

**Example:**  
So you can finally build once and sell repeatedly

**Why it works:**  
Paints the future
