### **HOW LONG DO YOU PROMOTE FOR:**

**In-Person**
- 6 weeks out: for events where people are traveling from out of state
- 2–3 weeks out: for local events

**Virtual**
- 1 week out: for online workshops

---

24 hours before the event, it always goes _mental_ and they get the most sign ups

That's the power of urgency and immediacy—people make decisions when they feel the window closing.

Use countdown timers, last-chance messaging, and scarcity to create that final push.
