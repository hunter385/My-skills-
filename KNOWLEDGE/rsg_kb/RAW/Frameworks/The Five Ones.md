# The Framework
---
![[The Five Ones.png]]




## Core Idea
The secret to scale is _scissors_

you have to get a whole lot simpler so it can grow a whole lot faster. simple things grow.

Taki claims that the Five Ones is the fastest path to a million dollars

simple scales. complexity fails.
complexity is easy to add, but hard to maintain.


![[rsg five ones]]

## **TARGET MARKET:**
marketing becomes easy when you only have one target.
- do I love working with them?
- can i help them?
- can they pay?


## **PRODUCT**:
- one thing we can look our “target market” in the eye and say this is for you and this will solve your problem
    - do you have a triangle magic model?
    - have you packaged your offer using the offer diamond to make an offer so good, people feel stupid saying no


## **CONVERSION:**
- one way we convert prospects into clients
- choose a conversion method that feels good to you and works
    1. Sales Call
    2. Offer Doc


## **TRAFFIC:**
- how do we get new leads into our world?
    - free: organic
    - paid: ads
    - partnerships


## **ONE YEAR:**
stay focused. dial this plan in. lock it in for a year.
tweak and optimize every cycle.
don’t whiplash the system.








## When To Use

- Starting a business
- Simplifying a business
- A Growth Plan
