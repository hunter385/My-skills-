| **Next step**           | **When to use + what I meant**                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
| ----------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **add to calendar**     | Use when the workshop is the main “conversion event” and attendance rate matters most.  <br>  <br>Meaning: This is a micro‑commitment. If they put it on their calendar, show‑up goes up, refunds go down, and your room energy is better.                                                                 |
| **book a call**         | Use when the next offer requires a conversation (high‑ticket, custom fit, limited spots).  <br>  <br>Meaning: The best “next step” is not “learn more.” It is “get on my calendar.” You are trading momentum for commitment.                                                                               |
| **join group**          | Use when you need a longer nurture window or you want community to do the heavy lifting (Facebook group, Skool).  <br>  <br>Meaning: You are moving them into an owned audience where you can keep delivering wins and building trust between events.                                                      |
| **join waitlist**       | Use when you sell out or you only open enrollment occasionally.  <br>  <br>Meaning: Waitlist is the “soft yes.” It lets you capture intent now, then convert later with a clear open/close moment.                                                                                                         |
| **upgrade to VIP**      | Use when the base ticket is low ($29–$100) and you want an immediate profit lever without changing the core workshop.  <br>  <br>Meaning: VIP is paid proximity. Examples: post‑workshop Q&A, hot seat, template pack, 1:1 review, or “office hours.”                                                      |
| **upsell / cross‑sell** | Use when you have an adjacent offer that helps them apply what they just learned without needing a call.  <br>  <br>Meaning: This is the “checkout bump” version for workshops. Examples: workbook + templates, a 14‑day implementation sprint, a tool bundle, a mini‑course, or a 30‑day community trial. |
