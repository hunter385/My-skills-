## Core Idea

### what’s your next belt goal?!

| **CASH COLLECTED**                                                              | **HOLD FOR 3 MONTHS**                                                               |
| ------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| based off of cash we got in this month not sales that will come later that year | can you hold that cash position for 3 months in a row… if so, you get the next belt |

### pacing
our 3 events: March - June - November
i want you to jump one belt per event cycle



## When To Use
- onboarding
- celebrate giving out new belts to the people who've gone to the next level at the 3 events in the year
