# Leadership — Cockpit
- How long has it been since you've announced and celebrated a company victory with the entire team, even if it's just you? (1-5)
- If you asked every member of your team to recite the mission statement of the company word-for-word, could they do it? (yes/no)
- Does everybody in your business know what the three most important objectives are for the company to grow financially? (1-5)
- Do you and your team understand how the mission of your business improves the lives of the customers you serve? (1-5)
- Do your team members clearly know what values they need to uphold if they are going to work for your small business? (1-5)
- Do each of your team members understand how they contribute to the overall success of the company? (1-5)

# Marketing — Right Engine
- If you asked your customers to identify the main problem your company solves, could they answer clearly and quickly? (1-5)
- Can you name the main problem each of your products solve for your customers? (1-5)
- Does your marketing paint a positive picture of what your customers' lives could look like if they buy your products? (1-5)
- In your overall marketing copy, do you make the customer the hero? (1-5)
- Are you using a copywriting framework on your various product pages that generates more purchases? (Yes/No)
- How long has it been since you've updated your website? (1-5)

# Sales — Left Engine
- Would you say that you or your sales team is talking more about the features and benefits of your product or more about your customers' problems and how your product solves those problems? (1-5)
- Do you or your sales team have an effective proposal template you use when you send your proposals? (Yes/No)
- Do you or your sales team have clear talking points you can memorize to help make selling easy? (1-5)
- Do you feel like you have to work hard just to get customers to pay attention vs. customers being engaged immediately? (1-5)
- Is it intuitive and easy for you to ask for the sale or do you hate asking for money? (Cringe vs. Love)

# Products — Wings
- How long has it been since you've released a new product to your customer base?
- Before launching a new product, does your team complete a vetting process? (Yes/No)
- When launching a new product, do you align your team around core messaging before the product launches?
- Do you have a subscription product that brings in dependable revenue every month? (Yes/No)
- How long has it been since you've made a list of your products (from most to least profitable)?
- Have you ever wasted time and money launching a product that didn't make the company profit?

# Overhead & Operations — Body
- Does every team member or contractor know what their critical priorities are at this exact moment? (1-5)
- Do you consistently encounter team members or contractors who are working hard but seem to be working on the wrong things? (Yes/No)
- Does every member of your team get feedback from their department head on a regular basis? (1-5)
- Do you have routine meetings that happen every week, month, and quarter or do meetings tend to pop up mostly as a way of putting out fires?
- Does everyone on your team get a quarterly performance review? (Yes/No)

# Cash Flow — Fuel Tanks
- If you are the owner of the company, do you pay yourself a salary?
- Are you ever surprised by a tax bill and find yourself without enough money to pay the bill?
- Do you keep at least five or six month's worth of overhead cash on hand as a rainy day fund?
- Do you have separate checking accounts for your operating expenses, profit, taxes, and your personal salary?
- Have you ever worried about whether you will be able to make payroll in the coming month?
- Do you have a plan for what to do with extra profit when the business is exceeding expectations?
