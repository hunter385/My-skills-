# **Cashflow Quadrant**

## **What This Is**
The Cashflow Quadrant, from *Rich Dad's Cashflow Quadrant* by Robert Kiyosaki, is a framework for understanding where income comes from, not how hard someone works or how much they earn.
The entire purpose of the model is to help individuals and businesses intentionally move from the left side of the quadrant to the right side.

## **The Four Quadrants**
Income is generated from one of four places:

### **E Employee**
You earn income by working for someone else. If you stop working, income stops.

### **S Self employed**
You own the job, but the job owns your time.

### **B Business Owner**
You own a system that produces income. People and processes create value without your constant involvement.

### **I Investor**
Your money produces income. Assets generate cash flow regardless of your time.

## **Left Side vs Right Side**

**Left Side: E + S**
- Income depends on time and effort
- Motivated by security
- Limited scalability
- You are the bottleneck

**Right Side: B + I**
- Income comes from systems and assets
- Motivated by freedom
- Scalable and leverage driven
- People and money work for you

**The idea is not to abandon the left side immediately, but to stop building things that trap us there.**

## **The Goal**
- Move from E and S toward B
- Use B quadrant cash flow to fund I quadrant assets
- Reach a point where asset income exceeds expenses

This is what "moving to the right side of the quadrant" actually means.

# WHAT DOES THIS MEAN FOR US?

**WHERE ARE WE NOW?**
RSG is currently an S Quadrant business:
- Revenue depends heavily on our direct involvement
- We are central to delivery and execution
- If we stop, income slows or stops

**A true B quadrant test**
If we left for 6–12 months, would the business:
- Collapse
- Stall
- Or improve?

Only the third option qualifies as B.

> MY BIG IDEA: What if we stop trying to turn RSG into a B quadrant business and instead create B quadrant businesses that support RSG? Instead of figuring out how RSG can work *without* Scott, we can build additional businesses around our S quadrant business to move to the right side.
