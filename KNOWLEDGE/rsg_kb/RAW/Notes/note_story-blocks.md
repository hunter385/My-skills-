# A CHARACTER
- what do your customers want as it relates to this course?

---

# THE PROBLEM
- what is a problem your customers deal with as it relates to this topic?
- this is the hook. what do people want that they haven't been able to get?

---

# CONTROLLING IDEA
- The main point of the talk
- Often the "controlling idea" is the SOLUTION to the "problem" section

---

# STORY QUESTION
- The question we want everyone to be asking themselves as they walk out of the room

---

# AGITATE THE PROBLEM

### VILLIAN
Is there a root cause of your customers' problems? Can you personify this root cause as a villain? What is the villain in your customer's story?

### EXTERNAL
What is the problem your customer deals with as it relates to this topic?

### INTERNAL
How is this problem making your customers feel?

### PHILOSOPHICAL
Why is it "just plain wrong" for your customers to be burdened by this problem?

---

# DEFINE THE STAKES
- Stakes make the problem even worse
- Stakes make it interesting
- What are the consequences if we don't do anything?
- What are the positive consequences if we DO something?

### WHAT ARE THE STAKES IF THEY DO NOTHING?
List the positive changes your customers will experience if they use your product or service.

### WHAT ARE THE STAKES IF THE DO SOMETHING?
List the positive changes your customers will experience if they change and do the plan.
