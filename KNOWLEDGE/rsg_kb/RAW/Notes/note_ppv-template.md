# PPV Template

**PRIMARY DASHBOARDS** → Core interfaces that surface the PRO system's source databases in context. They provide fast access to key info and actions, and streamline planning, execution, and review across work and personal life.

**HOMEPAGE 🏠**
- Alignment Zone
- Focus Zone
- Knowledge Zone
- Personal Space
- Work Space
- Connection Space

**ACTION・QUICK ADD** — Quick-Add buttons let you rapidly capture new actions. Add and categorize items instantly without opening the full database or navigating away from your current view.

Categories and Types buttons available.

**NEUROBIT・QUICK ADD** — Quick-Add buttons let you rapidly capture new NeuroBits.

**DAILY・QUICK ADD** — Each Daily page serves as a dynamic space for capturing daily information and reflections. Designed for quick information input and daily review.

**ACTION CALENDAR** — Displays and organizes uncompleted actions in the near term. View and manage to-dos across different time frames (*This Week*, *Today*, *This Month*).
