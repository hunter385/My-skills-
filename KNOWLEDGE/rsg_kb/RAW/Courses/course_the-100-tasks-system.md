# The 100 Tasks System

**Creator:** Martin Bell
**Linked topics:** business
**External URL:** https://access.100tasks.com/users/sign_in

## START WITH PROBLEMS
- Identify Problems and Trends
- Evaluate Problems and Trends
- Select Problem to Focus on
- Pinpoint Pain Points and Determine Jobs to Be Done

## PLAN MISSION
- Define Overall Vision, Mission, and Core Values
- Gather All Steps
- Streamline Steps

## ASSEMBLE CORE TEAM
- Master Founder Fundamentals
- Round out Founding Team
- Secure Mentorship

## COLLECT IDEAS
- Decide on One of "Three Horizons"
- Transfer Proven Business Models to Ecosystems of Future Growth
- Generate "Long List" of Ideas
- Distill into "Short List"

## DETERMINE BUSINESS MODELS
- Compare How to Innovate ("10 Types of Innovation" for "Short List")
- Compare How to Compete in "Blue Ocean" for "Short List"
- Compare Using "Business Model Canvas" for Short List
- Compare Using "Discovery"
- Rank Business Models on "Short List"
- Build and Adapt Proof of Concept of #1 Business Model
- Define Your USPs
- Assemble Focus Group and Follow "Lean Startup" Loop Until Achieving "Customer Validation"
- Ensure ESG Compliance
- Build Financial Model
- Create Pitch Deck

## DEVELOP MVP
- Specify MVP, Determine Tool Stack, Setup Lean PM
- Perform Legal Check of Business Model and Key Documents
- Calculate Costs for MVP Development
- Develop MVP
- Define Your Brand
- Establish an Online Footprint
- Create Design and Wireframes
- Finish Logo and Creative

## RAISE (PRE)SEED CAPITAL
- Consider Various Funding Options
- Calculate Required Funding Amount and Valuation
- Determine Non-Financial Investor Requirements
- Identify Relevant Investor Types
- Prepare and Pitch to Potential Investors
- Evaluate Potentially Interested Investors
- Secure (Pre-)Seed Investment

## BUILD FUNCTIONS
- Define Target Organization Chart
- Gather Requirements for Each Function
- Design Operating Model
- Incorporate Legal Entity
- Set Up Bank Account
- Set Up Accounting
- Define Central and Local Logistics Value Streams
- Select Payment Service Provider
- Register Trademark
- Perform Capacity Planning for Facility
- Set Up Content Production
- Build Supply Chain
- Organize Distribution
- Institute Sales Funnel
- Prepare Cross-Channel Marketing and Sales Strategy
- Ramp Up Facility
- Set Up Customer Care
- Prepare Tech Infrastructure and Security

## SET UP KPI REPORTS
- Define Top 20 KPIs
- Set Up Data Warehouse
- Prepare Daily, Weekly, and Monthly Reports
- Set Hiring Targets

## GO LIVE
- Stress Test and Bug-Fix Across Functions
- Prepare Press List
- Start KPI Reporting
- Conduct Launch PR Campaign and Paid Marketing
- Continue Testing and Bug-Fixing

# SCALE

## RAISE GROWTH CAPITAL
- Secure Growth Investment
- Set Up Employee Participation Program

## BUILD CULTURE
- Design and Track Hiring Process
- Foster People Development
- Create and Maintain Company Culture

## LEARN FROM DATA
- Navigate Using Daily, Weekly, and Monthly Reports
- Dig Deeper Using Ad-hoc Reports for Each Function
- Analyze Progress Toward Financial Targets
- Focus on Cross-Channel Marketing Mix that Works
- Analyze Customer Engagement with Product
- Re-design Operating Model According to Data
- Establish Proper Financial Reporting, Controlling, and Compliance

## OPTIMIZE FUNCTIONS
- Groom and Prioritize Product Roadmap
- Enhance UI/UX According to Usability Tests
- Boost Tech Stack's Scalability, Availability, Speed, and Security
- Eliminate Operational Bottlenecks
- Re-assess Suppliers and Partners
- Optimize Payment Mix, Fees, Checkout Funnel and Fraud Prevention
- Improve Management of Sales Funnel
- Optimize CAC VS CLV
- Enhance CRM
- Build Brand and Execute PR Strategy
- Improve Customer Care Processes to Maximize NPS
- Automate Important Manual Processes
- Accelerate Workforce
- Phase in OKR System

## BEST-PRACTICE COMPANY
- Define Best Practices for Each Function
- Implement Best Practices
- Implement Ongoing Knowledge Sharing
- Achieve Product-Market-Fit
- Constantly Evaluate Further Growth and Expansion Options
