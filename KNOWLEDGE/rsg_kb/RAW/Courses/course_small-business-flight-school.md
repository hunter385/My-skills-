## Six Parts of an Airplane

Every business has six major parts; if they work together properly, your business should run like a machine that makes money.

Six components:
1. Leadership (cockpit)
2. Marketing (right engine)
3. Sales (left engine)
4. Product Creation (wings)
5. Overhead (body)
6. Cash Flow (fuel tanks)

> In every decision you make as a business leader, you have to ask yourself how it will affect the airplane.
