### PUT THIS INTO ACTION

#### FOUNDATIONS
- Step 1: Define your sub-niche (Sub-Niche Playbook)
- Step 2: Build out your backstory for relatability (Better Backstory Builder)
- Step 3: Write down 3-5 polarizingly strong opinions for interesting content
- Step 4: Craft 3-5 short stories for relatable lessons

#### CONTENT CREATION
- Step 1: Choose content path: Leading, Discovering or Reporting
- Step 2: Fill out your own version of my improved content matrix
- Step 3: Practice iterating by combining opinions + styles
- Step 4: Practice copywriting one of the combinations
- Step 5: Load up Buffer with at least 5 posts (1 week)
- Step 6: Find a partner for the 4-step workflow
- Step 7: Begin to label posts in Shield

#### BUILDING YOUR TRIBE
- Step 1: Set up LinkedIn using bookmarks to activity pages
- Step 2: Find 2-3 people using LinkedIn Advanced Filters
- Step 3: Find 2-3 people using SparkToro
- Step 4: Find 2-3 people using Twitter Advanced Search

#### LINKEDIN LEAD CAPTURE
- Step 1: Create a better banner image (what do you do or who do you help?)
- Step 2: Updated, clean, professional headshot
- Step 3: Create a better tagline (Who do you help? What do you help them do? How?)
- Step 4: Update your featured section (free value with CTA to collect emails)
- Step 5: Re-write your about section (crisp & cohesive, further proof of competency)

#### BUSINESS WORKFLOW
- Set up inbound system (feature CTA on featured section)
- Set up outbound system (find target people, schedule interaction)
- Set up Google Alerts (choose keywords, templatize outreach)
- Choose your selling strategy: 80/20 or low-ticket tripwire
