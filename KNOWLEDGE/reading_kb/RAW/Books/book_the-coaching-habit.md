## CORE MINDSET SHIFT
**From:** Advice-giver, fixer, rescuer
**To:** help people think better by asking questions

The enemy of good coaching is the **"Advice Monster"**:
- Tell-It Mode
- Save-It Mode
- Control-It Mode

Tame it by asking better questions.

## 7 ESSENTIAL COACHING QUESTIONS

| # | Question | Purpose |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1. | What's on your mind? | Opens the conversation |
| 2. | And what else? | Forces deeper thinking |
| 3. | What's the real challenge here for you? | Separates symptoms from the real issue |
| 4. | What do you want? | Clarifies motivation and desire |
| 5. | How can I help? | Prevents over-helping |
| 6. | If you say yes to this, what are you saying no to? | Forces trade-offs |
| 7. | What was most useful for you? | Locks in learning |
