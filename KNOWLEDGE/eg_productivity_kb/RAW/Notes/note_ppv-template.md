**DAILY・QUICK ADD**
Each Daily page serves as a dynamic space for capturing daily information and reflections. Designed for quick information input and daily review.

**ACTION CALENDAR**
The Action Calendar displays and organizes uncompleted actions in the near term. View and manage to-dos across different time frames (*This Week*, *Today*, or *This Month*).
Switch between the three view tabs to manage your actions efficiently.
