# C. A. R. E. S.

### C
Connection - *Replenish through belonging and presence.*
Competence - *Rebuild confidence through mastery and momentum.*

### A
Accomplishment - *Reignite energy through meaningful progress.*
Achievement - *Celebrate momentum and milestones.*

### R
Recreation - *Recharge through movement and creative play.*
Relaxation - *Reset your nervous system with calm and stillness.*

### E
Enjoyment - *Move toward joy — not away from pain.*
Effectiveness - *Calm anxiety by regaining clarity and control.*

### S
Satisfaction - *Restore your body through reward and sensory renewal.*
Significance - *Reconnect to meaning and mission.*

### **C – Connection**
*Replenish through belonging and presence.*
- Call Emily, the kids, Dillon
- Share a meal or coffee with someone I enjoy
- Cuddle my spouse
- Go for a walk and talk — no agenda

### **C – Competence**
*Build confidence through mastery.*

### **A – Accomplishment**
*Making meaningful progress.*
- Complete one small lingering task (inbox zero, tidy workspace)
- Set + accomplish my daily 3 towards my goals
- Cross off one thing that's been nagging me
- Declutter one small area (desk, drawer, car)

### **A – Achievement**
*Celebrate momentum and milestones.*
- Share a recent success with someone who will celebrate it
- Treat myself to something symbolic (a nice coffee, a new candle)
- Capture positive feedback in my "Nice Things" folder

### **R – Recreation**
*Recharge through movement and creative play.*
- Go for a run
- Play pickleball
- Journal

### **R – Relaxation**
*Reset your nervous system with calm and stillness.*
- Take a long shower or hot tub
- Light a candle
- Meditate or pray in silence for 10 minutes
- Take a nap
- Go to a movie theater
- Ya Ya foot spa or Soyokazi
- Sit in the sun for 10–15 minutes

### **E – Enjoyment**
*Move toward joy — not away from pain.*
- Make myself a decaf coffee
- Listen to Hamilton

### **E – Effectiveness**
*Calm anxiety by regaining clarity and control.*
- Plan for my future
- Do a thorough mind sweep
- Clean my workspace or home
- Knock out a few simple errands

### **S – Satisfaction**
*Restore your body through reward and sensory renewal.*
- Take a slow morning or sleep in

### **S – Significance**
*Reconnect to meaning and mission.*
- Re-read encouraging words or messages I've saved
- Journal "why I do what I do"
- Reflect on the lives I've impacted recently
- Pray or meditate on gratitude
