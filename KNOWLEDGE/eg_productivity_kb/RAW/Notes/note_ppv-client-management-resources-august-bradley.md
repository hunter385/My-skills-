The template below offers a client "Company database". Note that since this training was created, the core PPV Pro system has been upgraded to include a People & Organizations database. That's now a single database that includes both people and organizations. In the new updated PPV Pro approach, Organizations would be a higher-level parent item, and People within the same organization would be sub-items under that organization. In the instance where there is no organization, people would just be entered on their own. This is all built into the current PPV Pro template.

So there's no longer a need for the client "Company database", as linked in the template and as shown in the workshop video. However, we're keeping it in the template and video in case you ever find a use for it independently of the primary PPV Pro protocol. Everything shown in the video can be done with the single database now.

All other aspects of the video are current and relevant for the current PPV Pro template and system. The "New Client (Database Templates)" below are relevant and valuable for client management.
