## **Design Custom Notion Covers**
In Canva, create a design sized **1500 × 600**

## **Create Section Headers as Images**
Instead of plain text headers in Notion, you can drop in **image headers** designed in Canva.
1200 × 200 or 1200 × 300 works well

## **Build Icon Sets That Match Your Vibe**
Design small square icons (300 × 300)
