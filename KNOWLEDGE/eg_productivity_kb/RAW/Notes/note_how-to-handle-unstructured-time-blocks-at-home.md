# **Framework: The 3 Types of Rejuvenating Time**
Your downtime should include **three categories**.

## **1. Restoration (quiet your nervous system)**
Examples:
- prayer walk
- time in nature
- reading scripture slowly
- journaling
- sitting outside
- breath prayer
These regulate your nervous system.
But they **can't be the only thing**, because your brain likes movement.

---
## **2. Creation (low pressure building)**
This is where people like you feel alive.
Examples:
- writing ideas
- sketching frameworks
- building systems
- learning something interesting
- reading a book that expands your mind
Not work.
But **creative play**.

---
## **3. Adventure (healthy stimulation)**
Your brain likes challenge and novelty.
Examples:
- lifting weights
- running
- playing sports
- building something
- going somewhere new
- hanging out with friends
These release dopamine in **healthy ways**.

# **The Porn Battle (very practical)**
Porn tends to appear when three things happen:
1. boredom
2. isolation
3. fatigue

The most dangerous moment is usually:
> sitting alone with your phone and no plan.

Your brain looks for stimulation.

So the solution is:
**design friction + design alternatives**

Examples:
- keep phone out of bedroom
- remove YouTube app from phone
- have a written evening plan
- replace scrolling with **specific activities**
