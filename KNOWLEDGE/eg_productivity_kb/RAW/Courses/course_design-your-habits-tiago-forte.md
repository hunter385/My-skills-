# Project Guide:
Your project assignment is to design a sustainable daily habit to accomplish something you have been wanting to do. I will walk you through the following milestones that are essential to creating a habit and making it stick:
1. Identify your new **keystone habit**
2. Choose an existing anchor habit as your new **trigger**
3. Choose a **reward** for completing your habit
4. Choose a **reminder**
5. State your **implementation intentions**
6. Make your habit easy to start by identifying your **small wins**
7. Identify your loopholes and **closers**
8. Modify your **environment**
9. Choose a **habit tracking** approach and tool
10. Select an **accountability** system
11. Evaluate your **results**
