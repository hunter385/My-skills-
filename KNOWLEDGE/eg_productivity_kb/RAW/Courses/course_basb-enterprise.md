# 1. Strategic Planning Layer
*Purpose: Setting direction and allocating resources*
- Environmental Scanning
- Vision, Mission & Values Definition
- Customer & Market Strategy
- Brand Strategy
- Strategy Formulation
- Innovation Strategy
- New Product/Service Strategy
- Goal Setting / Strategic Initiative Planning
- Strategy Communication & Alignment

# 2. CUSTOMER LIFECYCLE
*Purpose: Directly generating revenue and serving customers*

## 1. MARKET TO LEAD
*Goal: Generate interest and turn strangers into Marketing Qualified Leads (MQLs)*
*Buyer Stage: Awareness → Interest*
- Market Research & ICP Definition
- Positioning, Messaging & Channel Strategy
- Content Development & Campaigns
- Paid / Owned / Earned Media
- SEO & CRO
- Brand Monitoring & Sentiment Tracking
- Marketing Automation & Lead Capture
- Attribution & ROI Analytics

🔄 Output → Marketing Qualified Leads (MQLs)

## 2. LEAD TO SALE
*Goal: Convert MQLs into paying customers*
*Buyer Stage: Interest → Evaluation → Purchase*
- Lead Scoring & Qualification
- Owned Media Content Marketing
- Owned Media Direct Outreach
- SDR / Setter Process
- Sales Enablement & Collateral
- Discovery & Demo Calls
- Objection Handling & Follow-up
- Quoting & Proposal Management
- CRM Pipeline Reviews & Forecasting
- Sales Team Coaching

🔄 Output → Signed Contract / Closed-Won Deal

## 3. SALE TO DELIVERY
*Goal: Seamlessly onboard clients and prepare for delivery*
*Buyer Stage: Purchase → Onboarding*
- Contract Finalization & Internal Handoff
- Welcome Sequences / Client Onboarding
- Scheduling & Delivery Planning
- Success Metric Definition
- Asset / Info Collection
- Journey Mapping & Team Assignment

🔄 Output → Delivery Kickoff + Fully Onboarded Client

## 4. DELIVERY TO SUCCESS
*Goal: Deliver value and build long-term satisfaction*
*Buyer Stage: Onboarding → Value Realization*
- Fulfillment / Service Execution
- KPI Tracking & Reporting
- QA / Approvals
- Customer Success Management
- Feedback Collection (NPS, CSAT)
- Issue Resolution
- Retention Strategies

🔄 Output → Measured Success + Retained Client

## 5. SUCCESS TO LEAD
*Goal: Convert satisfied customers into growth engines*
*Buyer Stage: Value Realization → Advocacy*
- Case Study / Testimonial Gathering
- Review & Referral Program
- Cross-Sell / Upsell Opportunities
- Customer Community Engagement
- Ambassador & Affiliate Strategy
- Co-Marketing & Advocate Activation

🔄 Output → New Referrals + Expansion Revenue

## 6. SUCCESS TO MARKET
*Goal: Feed advocacy back into the top of the funnel*
- Past clients refer others → re-enter "Lead to Sale"
- Upsold clients re-enter "Sale to Delivery"
- Reviewers generate organic MQLs → re-enter "Market to Lead"

# 3. Enabling Processes
*These processes provide infrastructure, insight, and capacity to support the Customer Lifecycle and Strategic Planning functions.*

| Enabling Process | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Plan to Report | Budgeting, forecasting, KPIs, variance tracking |
| Order to Cash | Invoicing, payments, reconciliation |
| Procure to Pay | Vendor selection, purchases, payment |
| Information & Knowledge Management | SOPs, documentation, data access, systems |
| Partnerships Identification & Management | Identification, vetting, negotiation, activation, performance measurement |
| Resource Planning & Allocation | Capacity planning, team timeline alignment |
| Financial Performance Management | Pricing strategy, margin analysis, unit economics, ROI |
| Supply Chain & Asset Management | Inventory, vendor relationships, tools, and maintenance |
| Sustainability & ESG Management | Environmental impact, CSR, regulatory compliance |
| Risk & Crisis Management | Contingency plans, continuity plans |
| Hire to Retire | Talent acquisition, onboarding, performance mgmt, L&D, promotion, succession |
