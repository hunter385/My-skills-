3. Energy
4. Courage
5. Productivity
6. Influence

2. Control
3. Competence
4. Congruence
5. Caring
6. Connection

1. Personal Freedom
2. Beyond Fear
3. Sustained Motivation
4. Presence and Power
5. Reclaim your Agenda
6. Defiance
7. Advance with Abandon
8. Joy and Gratitude
9. Integrity
10. Love
11. Inspire Greatness
