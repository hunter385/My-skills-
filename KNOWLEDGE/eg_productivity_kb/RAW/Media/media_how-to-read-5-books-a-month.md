# 5 ways to read 5 books a month
---
1. Choose better books
2. Schedule reading sessions like you schedule exercise
3. Put rituals around reading
4. Do closing pushes when you get close to finishing your books
5. Take everything interesting off your phone.
	1. Make reading your default activity.

# How to become a serious reader
[How To Become A Serious Reader](https://youtu.be/CiOvKClmBaw?si=4UBJpBlM3NW3LgLk)
---
It's not about being "a reader" or "not a reader." It's about whether you've trained yourself to read.
This is similar to running—I wouldn't say "I'm not a runner" just because I tried to run a 5k after not running for 5 years.
I'm simply not "in shape" to run a 5k yet.
1. Start with books you're genuinely excited to read
2. Find an inspiring location for your reading sessions
3. Read 5 days a week for 10 minutes at a time. Maintain this schedule for 2 weeks, then increase to 15 minutes
4. Increase your reading time by 5 minutes each week until you reach 40 minutes. At that point, stop extending your time and instead gradually increase the complexity of your books
