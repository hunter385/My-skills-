## John Maxwell's Rule of Five
John Maxwell's "Rule of Five" is a daily discipline strategy where he commits to doing five specific things every day that move him toward his goals. These consistent actions compound over time to create significant results.
Maxwell's five daily practices are:
1. **Read** — He reads daily to learn, grow, and gain new perspectives
2. **File** — He organizes and files insights, quotes, and ideas for future use
3. **Think** — He sets aside intentional time for focused thinking and reflection
4. **Ask questions** — He asks thoughtful questions to learn from others and deepen understanding
5. **Write** — He writes every day to clarify his thoughts and create content
These five disciplines, practiced consistently, have been foundational to his success as a leader, speaker, and author.
