## Privacy Rule

This KB is private by default. The following operations are NOT permitted without explicit instruction:

- Quoting or paraphrasing LifePlan content in newsletters, podcasts, short-form videos, workshops, or any public output.
- Sharing personal context (family details, risks, mentors by name, financial details) outside of personal coaching prep.
- Cross-linking from rsg_kb wiki articles to lifeplan_kb articles.

Allowed operations:

- Internal reflection prompts (when Hunter explicitly asks for personal coaching, decision support, or LifePlan review).
- Pulling from this KB into Personal-folder projects in WORK AREAS/Personal/.
