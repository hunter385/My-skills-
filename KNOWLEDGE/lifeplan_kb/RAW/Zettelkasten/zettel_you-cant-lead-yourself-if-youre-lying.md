Ever tried to lead a liar? It's pretty much impossible to lead a liar. In professional settings, you fire a liar. Later I'll challenge you to do just that. Fire the dishonest version of you and hire a new you . . . an honest you . . . a you that always tells you the truth, even when it makes you feel bad about you. Besides, dishonesty leaks. Lie to yourself and you'll lie to others. FYI, if you have a hard time telling other people the truth when the truth makes you look bad . . . you're probably not being honest with yourself either. It works both ways.
