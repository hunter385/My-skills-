# **1. HEALTH**

## **Assessment Questions**

### **Physical Health**
- Are we sleeping enough?
- Do we have sustainable energy?
- Are we exercising consistently?
- Are we eating intentionally or emotionally?
- Are we treating our bodies like tools or temples?

### **Emotional + Mental Health**
- Are we emotionally regulated?
- Are we carrying stress?
- Do we know how to slow down?
- Are we emotionally honest with each other?
- Do we feel emotionally safe at home?

### **Rhythms**
- Do we have rhythms of rest?
- Are we constantly “on”?
- Do we protect margin?
- Are we operating from peace or urgency?

## **Reflection Prompt**
“What would a healthy version of us look like 12 months from now?”

# **2. HOBBIES**

## **Assessment Questions**
- Do we have hobbies that are NOT tied to productivity?
- Do we laugh regularly?
- Are we playful together?
- What activities restore us emotionally?
- Do we know what we enjoy outside of work and parenting?
- Are we creating memories or only managing responsibilities?

## **Reflection Prompt**
“What brings us alive that produces nothing?”

# **3. SPIRITUALITY**

- Is prayer reactive or relational?

- Are we building our lives around God or asking God to support our plans?
- Are we slowing down enough to listen?

# **5. MARRIAGE**

## **Assessment Questions**

### **Connection**
- Do we still enjoy each other?
- Do we pursue each other?
- Are we emotionally connected?
- Do we know what the other is currently carrying?

### **Communication**
- Are we defensive or curious?
- Do we resolve tension well?
- Are hard conversations avoided or handled?
- Do we feel heard?

### **Romance + Fun**
- Are we dating intentionally?
- Is there affection?
- Are we flirting?
- Are we creating shared experiences?

### **Alignment**
- Are we moving toward the same future?
- Are we united financially?
- Are we spiritually aligned?
- Are we building the same life?

## **Reflection Prompt**
“What version of our marriage are we currently building?”

# **7. FINANCES**

## **Assessment Questions**

### **Awareness**
- Do we know our actual numbers?
- Do we avoid financial conversations?
- Are we financially organized?

### **Alignment**
- Does our spending reflect our values?
- Are we unified financially?
- Are we building intentionally or reacting emotionally?

### **Emotional Health**

# **8. ENTREPRENEURSHIP**

## **Assessment Questions**

### **Purpose**
- Why are we building?
- What are we actually trying to create?
- What does “success” mean to us?

### **Sustainability**
- Is the business serving the family or consuming it?
- Are we building from calling or proving?
- Is growth aligned with our values?

### **Identity**
- Would we still know who we are without business success?
- Are we measuring worth through performance?
- Are we operating from stewardship or pressure?

## **Reflection Prompt**
“What kind of life is our business helping create?”
