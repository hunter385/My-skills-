## How the team operates

- **Dillon is the on-camera face** but is also a buyer. He doesn't operate the back office.

## The four pillars

The team is organized around four functional pillars (from `neoworld team.md` drawing):

1. **Streaming** — Dillon, Ian.
2. **Buying** — Dillon, Ian.
3. **Shipping** — Ian oversees, contractors execute.
4. **Coaching Calls** — NeoWorld University delivery. Customer-facing, not internal team meetings.
