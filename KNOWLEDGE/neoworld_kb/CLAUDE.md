# NeoWorld Knowledge Base

## What This Is

The knowledge base for **NeoWorld** — Hunter's second business, separate from Ready Set Grow.

NeoWorld is a Pokemon card streaming + sales business on Whatnot. Hunter is COO; his brother Dillon is the founder and on-camera streamer. Hunter runs operations, team-building, software tools, and is leading the development of a second product line — **NeoWorld University**, an education product for streamers.

The strategic thesis (from `Strategy/The Problem.md`): NeoWorld currently makes money when Dillon streams. To hit $20M+, the model has to shift to making money when *other people* stream. The growth move is multiplying Dillon — *"Build 5 people who are 80% as good as you."*

## Focus Areas

- **Operations** — Streaming, Buying, Shipping, Coaching Calls (the four functional pillars).
- **Strategy** — Business model canvas, 8-figure roadmap, Triangle Model, Growth Plan.
- **Team** — Hiring, role design, salary modeling (Keela + Nolan run the model; Hunter writes the proposal).
- **NeoWorld University** — the planned education product. Curriculum, Triangle Model, money buckets, target audience, magic triangle, landing page, waiting list.
- **Streamer Multiplication** — the central strategic problem: scaling streamers without losing per-stream economics.

## Relationship to Ready Set Grow

Ready Set Grow and NeoWorld are separate businesses. They share Hunter as an operator and they share his frameworks (Cycles + Cooldowns, 365, Cycle Rocks, etc. all apply inside NeoWorld too), but the audiences, products, and economics are different.

Cross-pollination is fine for internal work — e.g., applying RSG's Middle Method to NeoWorld's quarterly planning. But:

- NeoWorld content is NOT surfaced in RSG public outputs (newsletters, podcasts, workshops, short-form) without explicit instruction.
- RSG content is NOT surfaced in NeoWorld customer-facing material without explicit instruction.

## Privacy

Some NeoWorld material is operationally sensitive — financial models, salary discussions, team performance issues. Treat all NeoWorld content as internal by default. Customer-facing material gets the explicit-instruction filter.

## Librarian Role

Standard librarian posture:

- Ingest, summarise, write, and link without asking each time.
- Log every operation in `CHANGELOG.md`.
- Pause and confirm before destructive changes.
- Surface the strategic thesis (streamer multiplication) as the lens through which every operational decision should be audited.

- `RAW/Strategy/` — business model, growth plan, 8-figure framework, Triangle Model, The Problem.

## Writing Rules

Wiki articles follow `ABOUT ME/writing-rules.md` and `ABOUT ME/voice-profile.md`. NeoWorld writing leans more operator-internal than RSG public — fewer "Most pastors…" reframes, more "Here's the model, here's the number, here's the move." Same hook-open / framework-spine / action-close discipline.
