# The Framework
---

What do they need to believe about:

1. themselves
2. you
3. your program

to take the next step?




## Core Idea
design a workshop that answers those questions



> it’s been said that people need 7hrs with your brand to convert into high ticket…. yes, but i think there’s a huge difference between watching youtube for an hour vs being live and doing activities together live. I think the workshops accelerate this process by 3x
> Taki Moore



## When To Use
- designing marketing material
- designing a workshop to get them to believe
