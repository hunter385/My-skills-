## Core Idea
The people who are most naturally in flow, the people who are the most magnetic, they’re using formats that fit them like a glove. In this tool, out of the formats available, select where they belong for you.

|                    |              |                    |
| ------------------ | ------------ | ------------------ |
| teach on video     | vlog         | interview someone  |
| podcast            | react videos | q+a                |
| critique something | tiktok dance | ask me anything    |
| carousel           | case study   | rant               |
| go live            | storytime    | flip chart drawing |

**The Perfect Format**
1. almost zero prep
2. low effort
3. in your zone of genius
4. puts you in a flow state
5. demonstrates your authority
6. attractive to ideal client
