## Core Idea
Help them to identify early when they're going to work on Black Belt so it doesn't get pushed off and they quit.

Taki wanted us to identify 50 minute slots in our ideal week.

He suggested to set a 50 minute timer and then you'll be able to have 10 minutes to reset and set intentions for the next thing on your calendar.
