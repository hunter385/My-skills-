## Core Idea
Traditional lead magnets are dying.

<mark style="background: #FF5582A6;">People do not want:</mark>
- long PDFs
- ultimate guides
- courses
- 5-day challenges
- anything that feels slow, heavy, or complicated

<mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">People want:</mark>
- a fast result
- a tiny win
- low friction
- something useful in 2 minutes or less

The shift is this:
- **Old lead magnets** = information
- **Micromagnets** = instant outcome

A micromagnet is:
**A complete solution to a tiny problem.**

It works because it is:
- specific
- fast
- useful
- low friction
- outcome-focused

**Think:** small pain + small solution + fast result
