# The Framework
---
https://www.instagram.com/reels/DYUsjMCDa_w/


if you're selling to..

| **TYPE OF PERSON** | **THEY'RE MOTIVATED BY**                          |
| ------------------ | ------------------------------------------------- |
| rich person        | save them time                                    |
| students           | sell them their future dreams                     |
| men                | sell them on what their future success looks like |
| women              | sell them on what their future success feels like |
| kids               | sell to their parents                             |
| parents            | sell them on a break from their kids              |

## Core Idea
if you try to sell to everyone the same way,  you end up selling to no one


## When To Use
crafting sales
doing a sales pitch
