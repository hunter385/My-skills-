## Core Idea

#### **At any point in the program you’ll be**
1. **Green** - i’m clear and i’m moving
2. **Yellow** - I’m unsure and slowed down
3. **Red** - I’m completely stuck and not going anywhere

> Let’s make a promise to ask for help at **Yellow**
