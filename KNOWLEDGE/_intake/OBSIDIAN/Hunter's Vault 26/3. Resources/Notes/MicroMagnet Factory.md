## Core Idea
1. **PROBLEMS**: What are the broad category of problems that your customer deals with?
2. **SPLINTERS**: What are the tiny pain points that we can address inside that problem? Do not focus on the deep root cause. Focus on the obvious symptom the person feels.
3. **MICROMAGNET IDEAS**: what are the solutions we have to fix this problem?
