## Core Idea

The whole idea here is the goal of content is to get them to **BINGE** your content.

you want them to:
1. know
2. like
3. trust you

the more you can get them to binge, the faster they'll be to know, like, trust you and want to buy from you.

this doesn’t have to take years… it can take hours, days, or weeks. They don't have to spend months on your email list. It goes as fast as it takes them to cross the binge threshold.
