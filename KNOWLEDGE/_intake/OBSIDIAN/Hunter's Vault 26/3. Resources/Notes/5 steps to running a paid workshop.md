up: [[Workshop Frameworks]]

1. **SETUP**: pick the smallest problem you can fully solve in 90–120 minutes, then package it like a product.
2. **SIGN UP**: get qualified people registered 
3. **SHOW UP**: maximize attendance rate so you are not teaching to a ghost town.
4. **PAY UP**: deliver a real, provable win, then make the next step feel natural.
5. **FOLLOW UP**: turn the workshop into a conversion window, not a one-night event.
