# Project brief: NWU QR One-Pager (Insert in Every Order)

## What is this project?

Co-design with Dillon a QR-code one-pager that ships in every NeoWorld order to promote NeoWorld University.

## Goal

Ship a printable insert that drives qualified traffic from existing NeoWorld buyers into the NeoWorld University funnel.

## Audience

Current NeoWorld Whatnot buyers — the warmest possible audience for NWU.

## Key constraints

Must fit physically in the order. QR code must route to the NWU funnel.
