# Project brief: Website redesign

> This is an example project showing what a filled-in brief looks like. Delete this folder once you've seen the pattern, or keep it as a reference. Your real projects go in their own `-project` folders alongside this one within the work area.

## What is this project?

Redesigning the business website. The current site is three years old, looks dated, and doesn't reflect what we actually do anymore. The homepage still leads with services we stopped offering last year, the portfolio is out of date, and the contact page gets almost no submissions.

The new site needs to clearly communicate what we do now, show recent work, and make it easy for potential clients to get in touch. We're using Framer for the build.

## Goal

A clean, modern website that accurately represents the business and converts visitors into enquiries.

**Success criteria:**
1. Homepage clearly communicates current services within the first scroll
2. Portfolio shows our 6 best recent projects with proper case studies
3. Contact form submissions increase by at least 50% within 3 months of launch
4. Site loads in under 3 seconds on mobile

## Audience

Potential clients — mostly small-to-medium business owners looking for design and branding help. They're comparing 3-4 agencies and will spend about 90 seconds on the site before deciding whether to reach out. The site needs to be impressive quickly.

## Key constraints

- Budget: under $500 for any paid tools or assets
- Timeline: launch within 6 weeks
- Must work on mobile — 65% of our traffic is mobile
- Can't break existing SEO — the blog drives most organic traffic
- No developer needed — building in Framer so I can maintain it myself
